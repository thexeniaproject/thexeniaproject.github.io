What do we do?

A look into our operations

1. Find endangered fish

1,414 species of fish are endangered. First, we find a location and a species, and get to work.

2. Apply our technology

Next, we attach our novel blockchain-based RFID chips to the fish, in order to track their journey in the water.

3. Reduce marine threats

Finally, we determine the root cause of the endangerment and act to reduce its presence (e.g. illegal fishing).
